This timeline connects my education, internships, projects, and research interests into one path: from engineering fundamentals at Imperial College, to production systems at Tencent and Banying Suixing, to AI-focused graduate study and portfolio projects at USC.

<div class="timeline">
  <div class="timeline__item">
    <div class="timeline__date">2021 - 2024</div>
    <div class="timeline__content">
      <h2>Imperial College London</h2>
      <p>B.Eng. Electronic and Information Engineering. Built a foundation in signals, systems, hardware, software engineering, control, and machine learning.</p>
      <p><a href="/portfolio/deep-learning-fault-detection/">Deep Learning Fault Detection Research</a> became the main research-oriented project, connecting GANs, VAEs, sequence models, transfer learning, and anomaly detection.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="timeline__item">
    <div class="timeline__date">Jul. - Sep. 2023</div>
    <div class="timeline__content">
      <h2>Tencent AI Arena Internship</h2>
      <p>Worked on backend services and applied AI features for an education game platform with about 100,000 active programming learners.</p>
      <p>Connected Prefix Tuning, structured LLM output, and Policy Gradient reinforcement learning to product-facing game workflows.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="timeline__item">
    <div class="timeline__date">2023 - 2024</div>
    <div class="timeline__content">
      <h2>Systems and Hardware Projects</h2>
      <p>Built FPGA game logic, a C90 to RISC-V compiler, a balancing maze robot, and database join operators.</p>
      <p>These projects made the later AI work more practical: model outputs still need reliable systems, interfaces, runtime constraints, and deployment paths.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="timeline__item">
    <div class="timeline__date">2024 - 2026</div>
    <div class="timeline__content">
      <h2>University of Southern California</h2>
      <p>M.S. Computer Science with an Artificial Intelligence focus. Coursework and projects centered on machine learning, deep learning, LLMs, computer vision, and web technologies.</p>
      <p>Built portfolio projects in multimodal sarcasm recognition, forecasting, computer vision, and interactive ML visualization.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="timeline__item">
    <div class="timeline__date">Jun. - Sep. 2025</div>
    <div class="timeline__content">
      <h2>AI Voice-Interaction Terminal Internship</h2>
      <p>Worked on an AI companion and learning terminal using ESP32-CAM, MediaPipe, PaddleOCR, LLM response planning, and fish-speech streaming TTS.</p>
      <p>The project turned multimodal AI into a latency-sensitive product workflow: point at text, recognize the region, plan a response, and speak naturally.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="timeline__item">
    <div class="timeline__date">Now</div>
    <div class="timeline__content">
      <h2>Research Interests</h2>
      <p>LLM alignment, multimodal learning, speech interaction, efficient fine-tuning, RAG, and production-facing ML systems.</p>
      <p>The site now includes <a href="/research-notes/">research notes</a> and <a href="/portfolio/ml-visualization/">interactive ML simulators</a> to make these directions visible and testable.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
